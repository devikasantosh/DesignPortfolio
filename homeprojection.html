Home Projection Mapping

Experiences created with HeavyM

Living in a 90+ year old apartment in Pittsburgh with old art deco features, I decided to projection map my fireplace one day. Since then, I've been making rental-friendly temporary art in my apartment by occasionally mapping onto arches, decor, and I also have plans to begin projection mapping in my lobby soon!
