Binaural Audio

Audio experiences recorded with Binaural Microphones

A project from Experience Design (CMU ETC53613) taught by Ruth Comley.


ROLE Foley Artist
TIMELINE Fall 2025

Binaural Audio Recording

A gripping binaural audio immersive experience where you find yourself the accomplice to a murder, all while two overly curious cops happen to be lingering too close for comfort.


Experience Journey Map




Setup


Project Goals

  • A sitting audio experience
  • A single guest sits and listens to the experience happening all around them
  • Explore spatial sound
  • Tell a story with details, paint a picture with sound
  • Using theme - Emotional

Experience Goals

  • Immerse guests in the scene
  • Make them question rooting for the character
  • Instill anticipation and fear
  • Keep a comedic element

Final Experience

Binaural audio recording. Best experienced with headphones.

Challenges

Getting a strong narrative where the guest as the listener would not be asked questions or have to move around the room within the experience, but still have a "buddy" character guide them through it.
