Escape Room

A narrative-driven escape room using an Egyptian tomb set with sensors embedded on the floors and walls, and DMX lighting set up in the space. We were tasked with utilising the kiosk and the tomb to create an immersive escape room experience.


Space Provided

Tomb (left) and kiosk (right) sets provided for us to work with.




Experience Journey

Trapped in an Ancient Egyptian Tomb, you must solve the puzzles of the curse in order to escape. The lights dim throughout and when they go out it means the mummies will wake up to drive you mad for interrupting their eternal rest.


Estimated Length: 8 minutes

Actual Length: 10 minutes

Experience journey map.


Project Goals

  • Create a two player cooperative puzzle-based experience that explores the Cursed Tomb
  • One player is in the Tomb and the other person is at the Kiosk
  • Use a state map to track the users through the experience
  • Using monitors, touch screen, physical buttons, physical sensors, lighting, and speakers
  • Using theme - Ancient Egyptian Tomb

Experience Goals

  • Engage users to seek out the clues to solve the puzzles
  • Challenge users to navigate multiple types of pattern based puzzles
  • For group scenarios encourage them to work together as a team
  • Use lighting & sound to influence user decisions and emotions
  • Keep users engaged and curious without it being too difficult

Experience Walkthrough (General steps in the experience from the POV of the guest):

  • The guest walks into the tomb
  • The kiosk is lit up
  • The kiosk buttons are arranged by the guest
  • The tomb is lit up
  • The experience narration plays the riddles and the kiosk screen book also shows the riddles and hints
